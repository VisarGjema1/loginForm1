Frequently Asked Questions

How do solar panels work?

Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity using photovoltaic cells, which capture the sun's energy and convert it into electrical current.

What is the lifespan of solar panels?

Solar panels typically last around 25 to 30 years, with many manufacturers offering warranties up to 25 years. Their efficiency decreases gradually over time.

Can solar panels work on cloudy days?

Yes, solar panels can still generate electricity on cloudy days, although they are most efficient on sunny days. They rely on diffuse sunlight that can penetrate through clouds.
