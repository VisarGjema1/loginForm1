About Us

At SolarPower, we are committed to delivering sustainable energy solutions through solar technology. Our mission is to provide clean, reliable, and affordable solar energy to homes and businesses. History of Solar Panels

The history of solar panels dates back to the early 19th century when scientists first discovered the photovoltaic effect. In the 1950s, researchers developed the first practical solar cells, making solar energy a viable power source. Over the years, technology has improved, leading to higher efficiency and lower costs. Today, solar panels are a key component of the renewable energy revolution, powering homes, businesses, and even entire cities around the world. Our history is built on innovation and a commitment to sustainability.
